Uber Ride Analytics Dashboard
📌 Project Overview

This project analyzes Uber ride booking data to understand customer demand, revenue trends, driver performance, and cancellation behavior. An interactive dashboard was developed using Python, Plotly, and Streamlit to visualize key business insights.

❗ Problem Statement

Ride-sharing companies receive thousands of bookings every day. Managing driver availability, reducing cancellations, and improving customer experience are major challenges.

This project helps analyze ride-sharing data to support better business decisions.

🎯 Objectives
Identify peak booking hours.
Analyze revenue by vehicle type.
Study driver and customer ratings.
Understand cancellation reasons.
Analyze payment methods used by customers.
Build an interactive dashboard.

📈 Key Insights
Peak booking demand occurs during evening hours.
Premium vehicle types generate higher revenue.
Digital payments are more popular than cash payments.
Driver ratings are generally above 4.0.
Driver unavailability is a major reason for cancellations.
💡 Business Recommendations
Increase driver availability during peak hours.
Allocate more drivers to high-demand locations.
Reduce cancellations by improving driver assignment.
Promote premium vehicle categories to increase revenue.
